Questions

Frequently Asked

Is there a joining fee?

A one-time initiation fee of ₹2,000 applies to all memberships. This covers your fitness assessment, body composition scan, and onboarding session with an IronLeaf coach.

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes. Members may freeze their membership for up to 60 days per year with 7 days' notice. A nominal freeze fee of ₹500/month applies.

What are your operating hours?

IronLeaf is open Monday–Friday 5:30 AM–11:00 PM, Saturday 6:00 AM–10:00 PM, and Sunday 7:00 AM–8:00 PM. Platinum members have extended access.

Can I try before I join?

We offer a complimentary first session for prospective members. Contact our team to arrange your experience day.
